Help Us Chart the Path to a Resilient Future

Alianza encourages all Hispanics to enter and become an integral part of the civic life of the new places in Florida we call home.

Today we work across 12 counties along the I-4 corridor, serving a diverse membership of over 14,000 people. Focused on Caribbean and Latin American communities in Central Florida, our story is one of resilience, hard work, cultural pride, and sustainable growth.

Latino Resilience Hub Network

Our Latino Resilience Hub Network strengthens community leadership, climate resilience, civic engagement, and environmental stewardship across Central Florida.
